### Title
Recipient Account Not Bound to Signed Payload in Solana `finalize_transfer` — (File: `solana/programs/bridge_token_factory/src/instructions/user/finalize_transfer.rs`)

---

### Summary

The `recipient` account in Solana's `FinalizeTransfer` instruction is declared as `UncheckedAccount` with no constraint tying it to the NEAR MPC-signed `FinalizeTransferPayload`. Because the `process` function never validates the `recipient` account against any field in the verified payload, a front-runner can copy a valid signed payload from a pending transaction and resubmit it with an attacker-controlled `recipient`, redirecting bridged tokens to themselves and permanently blocking the legitimate recipient from claiming.

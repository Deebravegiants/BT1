### Title
Unelected Committee Member Vote Accepted by GOV Rule Before Protocol Version 11 via Mempool Bypass - (File: `eras/conway/impl/src/Cardano/Ledger/Conway/Rules/Gov.hs`)

### Summary

Before protocol version 11, the `GOV` ledger rule does not enforce the restriction against unelected committee members casting votes. The restriction exists only in the `MEMPOOL` rule, which is a soft filter that any block producer can bypass by including transactions directly in a block body. An unelected committee member who has authorized a hot key can pre-vote on governance actions; once the `UpdateCommittee` proposal that adds them is ratified, their stored vote is counted, potentially tipping ratification of treasury withdrawals, parameter changes, or other governance actions.
